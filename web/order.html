🌿 What happens after you adopt

You'll get a confirmation from Stripe right away. Every tree is hand-assembled and flashed before it ships — usually within a few days. A tracking number lands in your inbox the moment it's on its way to you.

🛠️ Rather grow it yourself?

The product's the easy path, but the source is open — flash a sprout onto any ESP32 you've got lying around, or raise a sapling on a Raspberry Pi Zero. Free for personal and community use.
